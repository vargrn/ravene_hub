Alternative system for Early Access verification

BioPunk: Phantasmagoria May 22
Edit ▥ ...

I’ve created an alternative system for Early Access verification, project updates, build notes, and other features based on the BioPunk hub and Mini App infrastructure.

The browser version gives the project a proper large-screen place: posts can breathe, images are not trapped inside a tiny app shell, and materials can be grouped into member access without being buried in chat.

For now, the priority is simple: make the site the main public surface for the project, keep the Mini App as a companion layer, and move everything important into an interface that feels like it belongs to BioPunk.

And now I’m going back to writing the continuation of the story.
