Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026. This policy explains how Ravene Hub handles account, membership, payment-status, and site usage data.

Ravene HubPrivacy

1. Who we are

Ravene Hub is an independent creator hub for BioPunk: Phantasmagoria and related digital membership content. In this policy, “Ravene Hub,” “Ravene,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to the operator of this website and its membership access system.

2. Data we collect

We collect the information needed to operate accounts, memberships, access checks, comments, and support. This may include:

  • Account details such as email address, display name, account ID, password hash, login/session metadata, and timestamps.
  • Membership details such as tier, payment source, subscription status, expiry date, renewal status, and access ledger records.
  • Payment-status metadata received from payment providers, including transaction/subscription IDs, checkout session references, wallet or blockchain transaction references where applicable, and webhook payloads required to verify access.
  • Site activity needed for security and functionality, such as session cookies, local storage, request logs, browser/device metadata, and anti-abuse signals.
  • Content interactions, for example comments or feedback that you choose to send.

3. Payment processing

Payments and subscription checkout may be processed by third-party providers, including MoonPay Commerce and MoonPay Ramps. We do not store card numbers, bank credentials, card security codes, private keys, or seed phrases. Payment providers may collect their own information to process payments, run fraud checks, comply with financial regulations, and provide receipts. Their use of data is governed by their own terms and privacy notices.

4. How we use data

We use the data above to create and secure accounts, verify login sessions, confirm paid membership access, unlock eligible digital content or builds, process comments and support requests, prevent abuse, debug errors, comply with legal obligations, and maintain the website.

5. Cookies and local storage

We may use cookies, session tokens, and browser local storage to keep you logged in, remember account state, run membership checks, and protect the site from abuse. Blocking cookies or local storage may prevent account login or paid access from working correctly.

6. Sharing data

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with service providers that help operate the website, hosting, databases, payment processing, fraud prevention, analytics, support, or security. We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and integrity of Ravene Hub, users, or the public.

7. Data retention

We keep account, access, transaction-status, and security records for as long as needed to operate the membership system, resolve disputes, meet legal/accounting requirements, prevent fraud, and maintain accurate access history. Some records may be retained after account deletion if required for security, compliance, payment records, or legal reasons.

8. Your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account data by contacting us through the support channel listed below. Some data may not be removable immediately if it is needed for payment records, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute handling.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect account and membership data. No online service can guarantee perfect security, so you should use a strong password, keep your email account secure, and never share wallet private keys or recovery phrases with anyone.

10. Mature content

Ravene Hub may contain mature fictional themes and adult-oriented digital content. We may restrict access to certain content based on account status, paid tier, age requirements, jurisdiction, platform rules, or safety/compliance needs.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the site, payment setup, or legal requirements change. The “Last updated” date at the top shows when the current version was published. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy or support requests, contact Ravene Hub through x.com/ravene_hub or through any official contact channel published on this website.
